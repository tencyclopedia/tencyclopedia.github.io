 10 Best Useful Apps - March 2016

Glovebox

get it on play store

Glovebox brings to android the best way of multi-tasking. Swipe without lifting the finger to select an application to open.

Macrodroid

get it on play store

MacroDroid is a task automation and configuration app that focuses heavily on usability with a simple UI and logical step by step process.

Goal Tracker

get it on play store

GoalTracker is a user-friendly app that helps you achieve success by tracking goals in your life. Use GoalTracker to set goals for the upcoming week, month or even a longer period, in areas like family, personal growth, financial budget, health, socializing, hobbies and career etc.

Power Toggles

get it on play store

An advanced power control widget for your android device.       

ES File Explorer

get it on play store

ES File Explorer (File Manager) is a free and full-featured file (applications, documents and multimedia) manager for both local and networked use.

Air Droid

get it on play store

AirDroid allows you to access and manage your Android phone or tablet from Windows, Mac or the Web, wirelessly, for free. Transfer files between your devices, and share files to your friends.

Appblock

get it on play store

AppBlock - Stay Focused is a free android application that helps you to block temporarily distracting programs on his mobile like Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and addictive apps like Talking Tom or Angela, etc. so that you can stay focused in your work.

Pocket

get it on play store

Pocket is your place to quickly save, discover, and recommend the best, most interesting stories on the Web. The next time you find an article, video or link you want to read or watch later, just save it to Pocket. It'll sync across your phone, tablet, and computer so you can come back to it whenever and wherever you'd like, even when you're offline.

SHAREit

get it on play store

SHAREit, the fastest cross-platform file transfer tool. Share all kinds of files whenever and wherever. No USB! No data usage! No internet needed!. Supports Android, iOS (iPhone/iPad), Windows Phone, Windows, and Mac.

Flynx

get it on play store

Flynx helps you open articles and links from all your favorite apps like Facebook, Twitter, Whatsapp, Google+, etc. in a revolutionary new way.
